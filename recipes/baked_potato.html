Perfect Baked Potato

Description

This baked potato recipe yields a baked potato that's crispy on the outside, but fluffy on the inside — just like the perfect baked potato should be. Plus, it's made with just a few simple ingredients that you probably already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium baking potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Scrub the potato, and pierce the skin several times with a knife or fork. Rub the skin with olive oil, then with salt.
  2. Place the potato in the preheated oven, and bake until slightly soft and golden brown, about 90 minutes. You'll know the baked potato is done when you can stick a fork in the potato and it easily pierces through the skin. If the potato is still hard in the middle, keep baking it for a little longer.
  3. Slice the potato down the center, and serve with butter and black pepper. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese over the top, if desired.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
